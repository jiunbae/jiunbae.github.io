How it works

Three steps. Then nothing.

No note-taking, no manual steps. Turn it on and get back to the conversation.

1

One toggle on the Today screen — or set a schedule so it only listens during chosen hours.

2

Just talk

Meetings, calls, everyday conversations. Calex transcribes on-device as you go.

3

Get your summary

Minutes after a conversation ends, a summary, decisions, and to-dos appear — ready to act on.
